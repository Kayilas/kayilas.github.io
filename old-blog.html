My Love for Legion Maining

Ever since I started my journey in MapleStory, I’ve been absolutely captivated by the thrill of Legion maining. There’s something incredibly satisfying about leveling up all your characters and watching your Legion level rise with each milestone. It’s not just about the grind; it’s about the sense of achievement and the joy of seeing all my characters working together.

One of the highlights of my Legion maining experience is definitely the progress I've made with my characters. Grinding through the levels can be a bit of a challenge, but the reward of seeing my Legion level go up is well worth it. I love how each character contributes to my overall Legion, and it feels amazing to see my hard work pay off.

Another aspect I enjoy is the community and camaraderie that comes with Legion maining. Connecting with other players who share the same passion for leveling up and improving their Legion makes the journey even more enjoyable. We share tips, celebrate achievements, and support each other through the grind.

Of course, it’s not always smooth sailing. There are tough moments and challenges, especially when pushing for higher Legion levels. But overcoming these obstacles and reaching new heights is incredibly rewarding. The sense of accomplishment and pride in my progress keeps me motivated to continue my Legion adventure.

Why I stagger my Monster Park

Posted on August 20, 2024

Howdey I sometimes (not commonly) get asked why I stagger my monsterpark runs by running one run on seven different characters!

The goal of this to me Is to get my BiS medals on as many characters as i can at once!

I also find getting the Juciy XP from each run of MP to be best used on multiple characters, not just one!

Of course, part of it is probably related to my lack of lotus/daimain bossing mules and things; I don't really need the upgrade, so I prefer to do it like this!

A good part of it is also the fact that I have the Antellion Medal for all my characters as well!

As I continue my journey in MapleStory, I look forward to more adventures, more victories, and more cherished moments. The game has been a fantastic part of my life, and I’m excited to see what’s next in this ever-evolving world.
